LEMONOID

WASD keys to move, Click mouse or press spacebar to shoot. Avoid the lemons and at the same time, shoot them. Try not to go to the edges or your player would not be able to shoot. Click on the mute/unmute button on the top-right corner of your screen to mute/unmute the background music. This is a never-ending game. You win based on your score and how long you can survive. Have fun!

SAVE THE EARTH - AN ANIMATION

Simply drag the speed variable to choose which speed you would like and relax as you watch my short animation.

This animation aims to show you how small little actions like throwing litter into the sea or planting factories that are harmful to the environment can hurt our planet, which causes global warming. To stop global warming, let's all SAVE THE EARTH!!!

3

BABYMOOGUS - A MISSION

Press arrow keys to move. You will first spawn in Navigations. To start, go to the computer at O2. It will launch Mission 1. For Mission 1, if the gun makes a sound different from the sound when the gun fires, it indicates that the ammo is at 0. Press the spacebar to reload. After completing Mission 1, head to the second computer in Admin. It will launch Mission 2. For Mission 2, avoid bombs and zappers. Collect apples to gain more health. If you completed both missions, you won! Have fun and I hope you enjoy!

Thank you and take care!
